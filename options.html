Customize your Pomodoro timer duration

You can adjust the duration of your Pomodoro timer by changing the value of the range input. The minimum duration is 1 minute, and the maximum is 60 minutes.

loading...

By default, the duration is set to 30 minutes. When you click the "Set Duration" button, the new duration will be saved in your browser and will be used the next time you start a Pomodoro timer.
